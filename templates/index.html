How LuggageShare works:

A traveler who has extra space while traveling, can offer space once their own luggage is set up in Look for senders section mentioning the From and To Country, free space. A person who wants to send a parcel by finding a traveler can contact the traveler directly on the Look for Traveler page. In case you do not find a traveler, you can upload your details. Any traveler who happens to register later for the same time will be able to see you and get in touch for taking your parcel to its destination.
